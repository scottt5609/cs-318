How to slam dunk

Getting ready

Stretch a few times, jump up and down a few times, but not enough to get tired.

The jump approach

Run slowly, then pick up speed. As you get close to the basket, quickly take your final 2 steps, and jump in the air.

Dunk

Bring the ball back, then punch the ball into the basket as hard as you can.
