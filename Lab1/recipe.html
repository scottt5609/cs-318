My favorite recipe

Oatmeal


Ingredients

  • 1 cup of oatmeal
  • 1 cup of water (warm, optional)
  • Medium sized bowl
  • Mixing spoon
  • Eating spoon

Instructions

  1. Pour oatmeal into bowl
  2. Add about half of the water
  3. Stir the mixture with the mixing spoon
  4. Keep adding water until the consistency is how you like it
  5. Eat the oatmeal with the eating spoon

This is a great recipe when you're away from home.
